Ice Driving

The BAC team mapped out various tracks on the ice before unleashing Mono on it, resulting in a thorough examination of the most extreme driving against a stunning wintry backdrop.

Production Car Record at Zolder Circuit

Briggs Automotive Company (BAC) has once again made history, as its Mono supercar has become the fastest-ever production car to lap the famous Zolder Circuit in Belgium.

Carfection Takes on the Isle of Man

Carfection has put together a truly epic 10-minute film celebrating founder Drew Stearne's time on the Isle of Man behind the wheel of Briggs Automotive Company's (BAC) world-famous Mono supercar.

Top Gear USA Experiences

Actor turned presenter Bill Fichtner gets behind the wheel of the Mono for the new BBC America show

Isle of Man Experience

A record-breaking 12 BAC Mono supercars descend on the Isle of Man for a festival of driving fun. As part of BAC's famous Experience tours, drivers enjoyed specially closed roads and a private track day at Jurby in the presence of like-minded enthusiasts. Epic.

Hillclimb-Winning Run

Drifting on Frozen Lakes

Watch highlights of BAC's hugely successful inaugural Mono Ice Driving Experience trip on the frozen lakes of Are, Sweden, in three of its One-of-a-Kind Mono supercars.

Evo Magazine's Fastest Ever

BAC Mono outguns the track-only, slick-shod McLaren P1 GTR to become the fastest car ever tested by evo Magazine around the Anglesey Coastal Circuit.
